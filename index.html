Spotted Hyena

(Crocuta crocuta)

It has a relatively long tail between 30 to 36 cm, with the hairy tip of it black.

Striped Hyena

(Hyaena hyaena)

It has long fur, which stands on end when disturbed or threatened.

Brown Hyena

(Hyaena brunnea)

There are not very marked differences between males and females, although eventually the males can be a little heavier.
